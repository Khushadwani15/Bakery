The concept

Built for boutique branding.

This site is structured like a modern bakery landing page: strong hero, product showcase, visual story, and a contact section that converts cleanly.

The feeling

Calm, premium, and feminine.

Rounded cards, soft gradients, and clean whitespace keep the design elegant. The pink shade works as an accent, not as a heavy background wash.

The motion

Little animations, not noisy ones.

Each section fades upward on scroll, images gently zoom on hover, and the hero card floats for a subtle handcrafted feel.

Image gallery

Integrated visuals across the full page.

The imagery is placed throughout the site instead of in one isolated gallery, so the whole experience feels connected.
